The selected timepieces will usually have automatic movements, sleek design, and excellent quality.

## STOWA Marine Classic 36 Roman

[![STOWA Marine Classic 36 Roman](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/2024-05-STOWA-STOWA-Marine-Classic-36-Roman--300x300.webp/public)](https://www.stowa.de/marine-classic-36-roman-automatic-basic-without-date-leather-strap-black-hand-stitched_2?mtm_campaign=thewatchresource_referral_flieger_klassik_sport&mtm_source=thewatchresource&mtm_medium=Referral)

**Specs:** 

- Movement: Hand-Wound
- Caliber: Sellita SW 210
- Power Reserve: 42 hours
- Case Width: 36 mm
- Lug-to-Lug Distance: 44.6 mm
- Price: Around $900

[Check Price](https://www.stowa.de/marine-classic-36-roman-automatic-basic-without-date-leather-strap-black-hand-stitched_2?mtm_campaign=thewatchresource_referral_flieger_klassik_sport&mtm_source=thewatchresource&mtm_medium=Referral)

The STOWA Marine Classic 36 Roman Hand-Wound pays homage to the precision marine chronometers essential for 18th and 19th-century navigation. STOWA, with its roots dating back to 1927, first introduced marine chronometer-inspired watches in 1939 and reintroduced them in 2002. This 36mm model captures that legacy with a crisp white dial, black Roman numerals, and a small seconds subdial at 6 o’clock, all encased in polished stainless steel. The temperature-blued steel hands not only add a touch of elegance but also enhance legibility, staying true to the functional roots of its predecessors.

This watch is powered by the hand-wound Sellita SW 210 movement, providing 42 hours of power reserve.. The movement's top-grade finish is visible through the sapphire crystal case back, showcasing the impressive Geneva stripes (also known as “Côtes de Genève” or even “Geneva waves”). With a thickness of just 9.8mm and a lug-to-lug length of 44.6mm, the watch offers a comfortable fit for various wrist sizes. Its 50-meter water resistance adds a layer of practicality, making it suitable for daily wear.

The Marine Classic 36's design is both timeless and versatile. The hand-stitched black leather strap complements the watch's classic aesthetics. When researching the opinions of current owners, the consensus seems to be that the watch looks and feels very high end, which was my thought as well. Whether paired with formal attire or casual wear, the Marine Classic 36 is a perfect choice for an everyday watch.
