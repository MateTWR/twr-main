Collecting watches can be quite a financially taxing hobby, especially if an overarching plan or goal is not set beforehand. It helps to have a vision, followed by applying a sound strategy to achieve it. Most importantly however, it’s imperative that you enjoy the journey! We are here to help with our favorite part, the research. Let’s get into it! 

The “Best Two-Watch Collection” is a frequently revisited objective, as we often wonder if it’s possible to have just two watches that are perfect for all occasions and cover everything we might need from our wrist companions.

We set out to create the best two-watch collection options for all price points. When deciding what timepieces to feature, we focused on the following requirements:

- Reliable movement.
- Versatile design: we should be able to cover all occasions from activities to formal events with the two chosen watches.
- Evergreen look: we’re aiming for the current/future classics.
- Similar price points for both watches to match their value.

A little disclaimer regarding the last point: generally speaking, we tend to see two approaches as far as 2-piece watch collections go. The first, where one watch is a low price “beater”, while the other is a much higher priced “nice watch”. The second approach is having two watches of comparative value but differing styles. We are hands down fans of the second approach! The reason is simple: we believe all watches in the collection should be worn and loved equally, and in our opinion the “beater” never gets the same love and respect as the “nice watch”, therefore, we level out the playing field by equalizing the value. Without further ado, let’s take a look at the best two-watch collection options!
