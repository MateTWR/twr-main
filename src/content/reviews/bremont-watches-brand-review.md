The origin of a brand, more specifically the country the timepieces are manufactured and assembled in, is always a focal topic when discussing watches. Swiss watch brands are always highly sought after, Japanese brands became very popular around the 1970s, and of course several German watch brands are widely recognized as well.

When it comes to Britain, we know Rolex was a British company originally, but we might have trouble naming other well-established British watch brands that are producing excellent quality timepieces.

This brings us to today’s topic of discussion: Bremont, a British brand that established itself as a very reputable and respected brand.

## The History of Bremont

Bremont was established in 2002 by brothers Nick and Giles English. Their father, Euan English was a former RAF pilot and engineer. The three of them shared a love of flying. On a fateful day in 1995, Nick was practicing with his father for an air display when their vintage WWII-era aircraft was involved in an accident, and Euan passed away. Nick had broken over 30 bones, and his recovery was uncertain. Thankfully he made it, and along with his brother, Giles, decided to transform the pain and trauma of the past. Combining their passion for aviation and mechanical engineering, Bremont was born. The business has made a considerable investment into in-house manufacturing—their timepieces are produced and assembled in Henley-on-Thames, England.

## Origins of the Bremont Name

As a continuation of the dramatic aviation events that eventually resulted in Bremont, the brand name has its own crazy story. In the 1990s, Nick and Giles were flying their biplane from the 1930s (you know those classic fixed-wing planes with two main wings stacked one above the other) over France. Due to some engine problems and less than ideal weather conditions, they had to make an emergency landing on a farm owned by a local French farmer, who happened to be a former aviator himself. The generous man offered to help the brothers by hosting them at his farm. To show their gratitude, they named the watch brand after the farmer, Antoien Bremont.

## The Evolution of Bremont Watches

When Bremont launched, the initial timepieces were understandably inspired by aviation and military, so the first models and collections were mainly pilot watches. One of their brand values is to create “mission-ready mechanical watches”, and I believe this truly captures the essence of the brand. I don’t see Bremont jumping on temporary design trends, they keep their focus on creating modern tool watches.

With this core focus never being deprioritized, Bremont was able to expand their watch lines, and have collections for most types of watches for different activities: field, pilot, dive watches, racing chronographs. On top of these, they have the Mayfair collection for their dress watches. We’ll get into more details about the watch collections below.

## Bremont and the British Watchmaking Industry

I don’t see a relatively new brand making this big of an impact on a country’s watchmaking world very frequently. 

First of all, all Bremont watches are manufactured and assembled in Britain. In 2021, they opened “The Wing”, a new state-of-the-art  manufacturing and testing facility in Henley-on-Thames. This facility was built specifically for machining, manufacturing, and the assembly of Bremont watches.

Aside from the initial inspiration by aviation, the brand has an additional focus on racing, particularly British engineering. Their Motorsport collection is inspired by vintage motorsports, and several pieces are tributes towards specific Jaguar racing cars from the 1960s.

As for a more modern angle, you might have seen Bremont as one of the sponsors of the Formula 1 team Williams Racing in recent years.

## Takeaway: Are Bremont Watches Good?

Bremont is a new luxury brand that is already very well established and respected. I was impressed with their early models, and since then the brand has done nothing but improve and further strengthen their place in the luxury watch market. The price range for the majority of their watches is between $3,000 and $7,000, so they’re definitely in the luxury category, but what I really appreciate about Bremont is the fact that they are not outsourcing their manufacturing for the materials and movements. This innovation, the choice to try something new and own, instead of opting for an easier “tried and true” is something that makes me excited about a new brand, and for this reason, I would highly recommend keeping an eye on Bremont. I foresee a bright future for the already accomplished brand. 

Bremont delivers beautiful, evergreen designs paired with very impressive technical specifications. As I’ve mentioned before, they have a very strong sense of identity, and are constantly innovating and expanding their collections at a very steady pace. This is very much aligned with Bremont’s mission statement: “Go beyond. Take it further.”

The Terra Nova collection is created for the modern explorer. These pieces are made out of 904L type stainless steel. For reference, watches are usually made of 316L stainless steel. Type 904L contains more chromium, molybdenum, and nickel than 316L. What we need to know as the main takeaway  is that the 903L type is more corrosion resistant than the 316L.

The Terra Nova collection is inspired by the military pocket watches of the early 20th century. They encompass essential functionality with unexpected style and elegance.

The new collection has Bremont’s new logo, the “Wayfinder”, reiterating the brand’s dedication to adventure and exploration.
