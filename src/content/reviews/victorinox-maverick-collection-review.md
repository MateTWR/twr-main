## Victorinox Maverick Collection

![WAT\_241865\_B\_1-1-min](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/elementor-thumbs-WAT_241865_B_1-1-min-e1656291587580-pqwp5287udjh0dq70ztcqhhpphxw9ao43mgsxr0cq8.jpg/public "WAT_241865_B_1-1-min")

The Victorinox Maverick Collection reflects the daring nature of the deep-sea diver, for whom exact timing might be the difference between life and death. The unidirectional bezel on these fashionable timepieces, originally designed to allow divers to record precise underwater times, has become a symbol of the explorer and the trailblazer, calibrating daily difficulties and chores. Whether used above or below the water, the technically flawless and aesthetically gorgeous Maverick watches provide the control and accuracy needed to manage modern life. It also has sapphire crystal for maximum durability.

All watches in the Maverick collection come with a very reliable quartz movement.   
There are chronographs versions available with both leather bands and stainless steel bracelets.  
The non-chronograph versions display military time, and they are also available with leather bands, and stainless steel bracelets, additionally, there is a silicone strap option as well for salt-water activities.

### Victorinox Maverick

Starting out strong with the Victorinox Swiss Army Maverick, we are looking at a definite winner: reliable quartz movement, brushed stainless steel case (for that understated, timeless look), and a beautiful deep blue dial behind a sapphire crystal dial window. In the true spirit of classic Swiss watchmaking, it is a practical choice: wear it to the office, take it on a hiking adventure, or plunge into the ocean wearing the Maverick on your wrist.

A watch that breaks all the rules: a risk-taker. The Victorinox Maverick watches defy the status quo and triumphs whether diving, sailing, or engaging in any extreme sport. Additionally, the multipurpose unidirectional bezel establishes a new benchmark for limitless living.

#### Maverick & Maverick Chrono capture the adventurous spirit of the deep sea diver

These accurate timepieces are tools of trust and high-quality performance, just like every Victorinox Swiss Army Knife is a weapon of uncompromising practicality.

The Maverick Collection, designed and manufactured in Switzerland, incorporates a quartz movement, a mix of brushed and polished materials, an antireflective sapphire crystal, and incorporated crown protection for maximum durability. A 5-year warranty+ is another evidence of the brand's commitment to quality.

"By definition, the Maverick Collection selects a different route to the norm."
