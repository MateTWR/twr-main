## Victorinox Airboss Collection

![Victorinox Airboss automatic movement](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/elementor-thumbs-Screen-Shot-2022-06-25-at-11.24.43-AM-pqu9ggo0noe7uuv3cjcrb1j92keyxn6vfwrdxjbwc0.png/public "Victorinox Airboss automatic movement")

Victorinox developed the Airboss Mechanical Collection with dependability and accuracy in mind. The design of the watches was inspired by the real Air Boss, who oversees the countdown on an aircraft carrier. Every takeoff and landing is scheduled by the Air Boss, which calls for extreme precision. And the Victorinox Airboss watch will provide you with exactly that.

There is no exact definition of pilot watches but the following characteristics are commonly found in pilot watch collections, and the Victorinox Airboss line definitely delivers in these categories:

- Large and Legible Dial: Pilots need to be able to swiftly and easily discern the time in adverse weather, at night, etc. because they have a lot to focus on when flying. Therefore, pilot watches usually have big dials that often have dark hues and contrasting digits.
- Triangle of orientation at 12 o'clock.
- Large Arabic numerals. 
- Luminosity is essential for improving reading during nighttime flights.
- Large crowns were essential since the watch had to be operated while the pilot was still wearing gloves. As a tribute to the earlier times, it is still a classic feature today.
- Additional bezel markings: The indications helped pilots calculate things like fuel usage and distance.
