## Hamilton Khaki Navy Collection

![Hamilton Khaki Navy Collection](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/2022-05-10816-min-1024x767.jpeg/public)

Looking for a reliable, good looking but also functional new dive watch? Allow us to take you on a tour of the most popular Hamilton underwater timepieces. The Hamilton Khaki Navy line is inspired by maritime pioneers and designed with ocean explorers in mind. These watches are at home in- or under the water, as well as on land. Thanks to their sturdy build, luminous displays and the assurance of Hamilton excellence, this collection will not disappoint.

## Scuba & Frogman & BelowZero

Hamilton has 3 dive watch variations:

- The [Khaki Navy Scuba](#Scuba). If you are looking for a classic, timelessly designed dive watch, this is it. The Scuba collection from Hamilton features time pieces for all budgets, thanks to a wide range of movements available. 
- The [Frogman](#Frogman): During WWII, divers used the original Hamilton Frogman, which was built for the US Navy's 'Frogman' unit. These watches are more military-style, as opposed to the classic, understated Scuba counterpart. 
- [BeLOWZERO](#belowzero). Hamilton's Khaki Navy line, inspired by maritime pioneers, blends efficiency and flair to keep you on time both above and below the sea.

## Hamilton Khaki Navy: Scuba

The Khaki Navy Scuba has been a part of the line for some years, and it comes in quartz and automatic variants, with a variety of designs. 

Hamilton’s newest Khaki Navy watches feature innovative materials and technology, bringing high-tech style to the high seas. 

The Khaki Navy Scuba is Inspired by the design and function of traditional dive watches, while incorporating modern elements to make it a truly classic timepiece. Its unidirectional rotating bezel, excellent build, and simple readability provide consistent functionality, while essential features take it to the next level.

The bezel's colored ring, which matches the dial’s color, is composed of ceramic, one of watchmaking's most advanced materials. The ceramic is scratch and corrosion resistant, ensuring that the watch's exquisite state is not jeopardized by factors such as pebbles, sand, or seawater. Wearers may keep track of their dive time with a Super-LumiNova dot at 12 on the bezel.

## Hamilton Khaki Navy: Frogman

The original Hamilton Frogman was designed for the United States Navy's 'Frogman' squad and was worn by divers during WWII. Later, it was featured in the 1951 hit film 'The Frogmen.' Since then, about 70 years have passed, and watchmaking technology has advanced significantly. The essential elements that made the watch haven't changed: the unidirectional rotating bezel, exceptional water resistance, and underwater lighting.

## Hamilton Khaki Navy: BeLOWZERO

Are you up for a trip to the stars or a voyage through time? The distinctive style of Hamilton’s Khaki Navy BeLOWZERO has appeared in popular movies such as "The Martian" and "TENET." A redesigned titanium casing gives this high-performance watch even more lightweight adaptability. 

Hamilton's Khaki Navy line, inspired by maritime pioneers, blends efficiency and flair to keep you on time both above and below the sea. The Khaki Navy BeLOWZERO is distinctive in its design and is an eye catcher with its amazing 46mm redesigned casing made of titanium, and a matching black rubber strap. The new BeLOWZERO is 30% lighter than the original while maintaining a comfortable and secure fit, ideal for someone who leads an active lifestyle. The hands and numerals on the watch's sleek black dial are coated in dark gray Super-LumiNova®, which improves readability in low light.
