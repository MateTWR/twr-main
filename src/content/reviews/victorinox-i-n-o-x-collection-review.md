## Victorinox I.N.O.X. Collection

![Victorinox I.N.O.X. Collection](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/elementor-thumbs-WAT_241834_B1-min-2-pqlwbd7skrr2m25rqewxer70d2spf8si9gtw4hj4og.jpg/public "Victorinox I.N.O.X. Collection")

The watches in the Victorinox I.N.O.X. collection are the perfect companions for all activities as they're strong and resilient. They keep up with an active lifestyle, from squash court battles to temperature shocks in a hot sauna and ice plunge.

This line includes timepieces made from different materials, movements, each with its own personality but adhering to the same ideals of durability and design. These watches, which are made of durable materials like carbon composite, titanium, and stainless steel (316L), are ideal for anything you might be up to during the year: ocean swim, a hike, or a day at the office.

## Victorinox I.N.O.X. Professional Diver

It is powerful, sturdy, and athletic, and it adapts to every environment, especially in salty, watery, and windy conditions. A watch that can stand up to the elements.

In the depths of the ocean, the blue luminous minutes hand and capsule at 12 o'clock provide optimum reading, while the green glow of the other components guarantees that they stay visible in the dark.

The I.N.O.X. Professional Diver is a certified diving watch that exceeds the required parameters, meeting the stringent NIHS 92-11 Swiss watchmaking standards. It has passed a total of 130 tests that were designed to ensure its resilience and performance. The I.N.O.X.'s faultless solidity and capacity to satisfy the fundamental demands of professional divers are confirmed by these tests.

##   
  
Victorinox I.N.O.X. Carbon

The I.N.O.X. Carbon Collection explores the realm of military commandos and night survival skills. Thanks to high-grade green Super LumiNova®, the generously proportioned numerals and hands are easily discernible even in complete darkness. Green tracers are intricately weaved into the handwoven paracord strap to match the reflective patterns on the accessory bumper. With the help of a novel closure method, the watch is attached in record time, in true commando style. A second rubber strap reawakens the owner's zest of exploration.

The I.N.O.X. Carbon is made of a carbon composite that has shielded space shuttles from reentry temperatures of over 1,260°C. It has exceptional performance and durability. The watch, which is extremely light, successfully blends structural strength, scratch resistance, and hypoallergenic qualities. It's ideal for the young urban man searching for a high-tech, inventive wristwatch that keeps up with his fast-paced lifestyle.

##   
Victorinox I.N.O.X. Mechanical

The I.N.O.X. Mechanical is the newest addition to the Victorinox I.N.O.X. collection. This innovation replaces quartz with mechanical movement in a finely built delicate mechanism. The dial's complex guilloche pattern is not only a distinctive, timeless element, but it's also meant to resemble the handle of a Swiss Army Knife. To cap it off, Victorinox has invented a hardwood strap that is the first of its type. It's the perfect invention that goes hand in hand with our environmental mission. It's strong, robust, and fashioned into a leather base.
