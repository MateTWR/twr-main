## The History of UNDONE

UNDONE was founded by designer Michael Young in 2016 with the help of a Kickstarter campaign. It was envisioned as a mid-range brand with luxury preferences, and Young's distinctive minimalist, streamlined aesthetic was prominently included into the designs.

Michael asked the following question, thinking about the degrees of customization given by car companies and the high-priced and time-consuming procedure of customizing luxury watches: 

“What if a new business could provide similar services via a website that allows for client personalization at a lower cost?”

UNDONE highlights a trend that has been sweeping the menswear business for the past decade: personalization and character cost a lot of money, but you don't want to settle for quick fashion, which won't last as long as a heritage piece and is meant to look like everything else on the market. UNDONE, on the other hand, adds individuality to the mix without adding a Rolex price tag.

## The Quality of UNDONE Watches

All UNDONE timepieces are produced in Hong Kong. UNDONE maintains tight control over its supply chain and can design and build timepieces in-house.   
The steel casings are made of durable 316L steel.

Each watch is finished with a Lexan Polycarbonate crystal. UNDONE watches are assembled in-house at their Hong Kong headquarters.

## UNDONE Watch Movements

UNDONE is using both quartz and mechanical movements in their timepieces, and both are provided by the dependable Japanese watch company, Seiko.

## Takeaway: Are UNDONE Watches Worth the Money?

UNDONE watches are available at a great price point and they're coming with very dependable movements. Additionally, the UNDONE website created a great user experience for customizing your timepiece.  

This is a combination you can't really beat, therefore our answer is: Yes, UNDONE watches are good purchases!

### Urban Collection

The popular UNDONE Urban watches are custom-made vintage-inspired 40mm mecha-quartz timepieces.

Each watch in the Urban line is ideally suited for day-to-day wear thanks to its 40mm size, which is perfect for almost all wrist sizes.

A collection of classic dial designs, rich in complex features and textures and presented in a color palette that commands respect and class. A classic timepiece with understated flair for the modern gent.
