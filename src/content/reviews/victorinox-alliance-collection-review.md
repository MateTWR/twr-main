## Victorinox Alliance Collection

![Victorinox Alliance Collection](https://imagedelivery.net/X-EqW8t9MkEGniKrzeQMdA/elementor-thumbs-WAT_241823_B1-min-scaled-pqr47qvbfbodqwisicrd3sd2l616sawpm1b2ybx2sw.jpg/public "Victorinox Alliance Collection")

The Victorinox watches in the Alliance collection are subtle yet powerful. At first, it goes unnoticed and never screams for attention. However, a closer look reveals elements that demonstrate perfect confidence under the surface. This series combines the finest of Victorinox design, technology, and craftsmanship in the most beautiful way possible.

The design of the Alliance watches is somewhat surprising. Victorinox watches are usually military inspired timepieces. The Alliance watches have a minimalist look that makes them an excellent choice for your "everyday beater".

With a water resistance of 10 ATM/100 M/330 FT and a rigorous Victorinox testing procedure that takes each device through over 100 quality control tests before being on the market, durability is not an issue. Best of all, the Alliance watches com with a 5-year warranty, so you can be confident that it will last a long time!

###   
Victorinox Alliance, Mechanical Movement, 40 mm

The Victorinox Alliance collection has the Mechanical model with automatic movement with 40 mm case diameter, which makes it a great fit for all wrist sizes. The true timeless look is paired with the ETA 2892 automatic movement, providing a 42 hour power reserve. The exhibition case back provides a sneak peek into the inner workings of the movement, and the calendar window is placed at the 6 o’clock position on the dial–giving it a well balanced look that is very pleasing to the eye. It’s quite simply the whole package, a choice you will not come to regret.
