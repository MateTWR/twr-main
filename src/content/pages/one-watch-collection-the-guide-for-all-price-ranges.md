Every once in a while, upon thorough consideration and contemplation, a final decision must be made.  Today is one of those days. We're going to try to address one of the toughest puzzles, as far as watches are concerned: how to choose **the one watch that “has everything”**. 

The intriguing, mildly anxiety-inducing "one watch collection" question.

While the absolute dream is to own a collection that spans all brands, styles, and price points, in reality it is infinitely more practical to use the minimalist approach, as with most things. If you are looking for the perfect watch that checks all the boxes, we are pleased to be at your service.

We collected watches for different price categories making sure they meet our requirements:

- Reliable movement.
- The watch can be dressed up or down: This chosen watch for your “one watch collection” should be perfect for any situation: brunch, meeting, weekend getaway. 
- The capacity to be an “everyday beater watch”: To ensure the watch's lifespan, it must have a durable stainless steel or comparable material casing. 
- 100-meter minimum water resistance. Heavy rain, pool party, surprise swim in the ocean - none of these should be an issue.
